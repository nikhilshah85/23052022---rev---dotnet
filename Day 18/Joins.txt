Inner Join - Equi Join
	this will give all the common records from both the tables
	(common records referd to Columns mentioned in = sign)

Left Join - will give you all the records from left table, and common records from right 				table		
		(common records referd to Columns mentioned in = sign)


Right Join - will give you all the records from right table, and common records from left 				table		
		(common records referd to Columns mentioned in = sign)


Full Join - equal as well as non equal records
